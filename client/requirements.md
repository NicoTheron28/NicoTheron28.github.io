## Packages
framer-motion | For smooth page transitions and scroll animations
html2canvas | To save the timetable as a photo
embla-carousel-react | For the iOS-style scroll wheel time picker
lucide-react | For icons (download, share, scroll arrows)

## Notes
- Start time calculation must use seconds precision to avoid rounding errors.
- End time is fixed at 13:50.
- Walking time is 4 minutes (deducted from total time, not displayed on final schedule).
- Break is 30 minutes fixed between Period 4 and 5.
- The scroll wheel picker needs to be touch-friendly.
